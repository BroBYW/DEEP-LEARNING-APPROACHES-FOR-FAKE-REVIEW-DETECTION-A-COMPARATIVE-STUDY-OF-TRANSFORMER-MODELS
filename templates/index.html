How It Works

Our AI-powered fake review detector uses a sophisticated RoBERTa (Robustly Optimized BERT Pretraining Approach) model that has been fine-tuned on thousands of authentic and fake reviews. The system analyzes various linguistic patterns, writing styles, and content characteristics to determine the authenticity of reviews.

The model can detect three types of content:

  • Genuine Reviews (label): Authentic reviews written by real customers
  • Computer Generated (CG): Reviews created by AI or automated systems (Fake)
  • Original Review (OR): Authentic original reviews written by real customers
Disclaimer: This AI tool is designed to assist in identifying potentially fake reviews but may not be 100% accurate. The results should be used as a guide and not as definitive proof. Always use your judgment and consider multiple factors when evaluating review authenticity.
